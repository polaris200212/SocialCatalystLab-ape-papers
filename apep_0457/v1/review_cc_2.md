## Summary of Changes Since Round 1

The paper has been substantially improved:

1. **Fixed RDD table**: RDD estimates are now properly populated (-0.713 for total, -0.814 for tertiary) with appropriate conventional and robust p-values.
2. **Internal consistency**: All pre-trends claims are now honest and consistent across sections (Introduction, Identification, Results, Conclusion).
3. **Treatment measurement**: Extensive new discussion of why 2017 inventory is a reasonable proxy, with three mitigating arguments.
4. **Canton × year FE**: New specification absorbing cantonal trends shows the effect *increases* to -4.8%, arguing against the structural transformation interpretation.
5. **FTE and Establishments results**: Appendix now reports actual estimates (not just descriptions).
6. **Map added**: Geographic distribution of treatment clearly shown.
7. **Sectoral event study**: Split into panels for clarity.
8. **Improved prose**: Opening now starts with the policy shock, not generic throat-clearing. Results narrated with substance, not "Column X shows Y."
9. **RI caveat**: Now notes that RI tests the sharp null but does not address confounding.
10. **Bacon decomposition text**: No longer falsely claims TWFE and CS coincide.

## Remaining Issues

**Minor:**
- The conclusion could note the canton × year FE result more prominently, as it's the most compelling evidence against the structural interpretation.
- Some table notes could be more precise about what "Post" means (now corrected in Table 2 but not everywhere).

**The core identification challenge remains:** Pre-trends are real, CS pre-test rejects, near-threshold DiD is null, and placebo cutoffs are significant. The paper handles this honestly, but the fundamental tension between the strong TWFE results and the weak identification diagnostics limits the paper to a "descriptive" rather than "causal" contribution. This is appropriate and the paper's framing now reflects this.

## Assessment

The paper is now ready for external review. The identification challenges are transparently reported, the robustness battery is comprehensive, and the additions (canton × year FE, map, FTE/establishments) strengthen the contribution.

DECISION: MINOR REVISION
