## PART 2: CONSTRUCTIVE SUGGESTIONS

The paper is now methodologically sound and well-written. To further strengthen for a top journal:

1. **Industry heterogeneity:** Could still add analysis by sector (though data constraints acknowledged)

2. **Minimum detectable effect:** Calculate and report MDE given sample size - helps readers interpret the null

3. **External validity:** Brief discussion of whether findings generalize to larger MW increases (e.g., $15 policies)

4. **Policy implications:** Could strengthen the conclusion with clearer policy takeaways

These are enhancements, not requirements.
