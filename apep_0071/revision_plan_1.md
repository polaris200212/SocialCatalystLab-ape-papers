### Key Concerns Raised

1. **Identification Strategy (All Reviewers)**
   - Canton border RD identifies "canton bundle" not just energy law
   - Other canton-level differences (taxes, party systems, culture) confound the effect
   - Placebo discontinuities on unrelated referendums undermine causal interpretation

2. **Inference (All Reviewers)**
   - Municipality-level SEs understate uncertainty
   - Treatment is at canton level (5 treated) with few border segments
   - Need border-segment clustering or randomization inference

3. **Design-Diagnostics Mismatch**
   - Some figures/diagnostics use pre-correction sample
   - Should align all visuals with corrected sample construction

4. **Over-interpretation**
   - Claims of "negative feedback" stronger than evidence supports
   - Should be more cautious given placebo discontinuities
