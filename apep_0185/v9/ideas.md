# Research Ideas (Revision of APEP-0193)

## Idea 1: Social Networks Transmit Minimum Wage Shocks (REVISION)
**Policy:** State-level minimum wage variation (2012-2022), including Fight for $15 movement
**Outcome:** County-level employment from Quarterly Workforce Indicators (QWI)
**Identification:** Shift-share IV using Facebook Social Connectedness Index (SCI) shares × out-of-state MW shocks
**Why it's novel:** Population-weighted network exposure captures information volume, not just network share
**Feasibility check:** Confirmed: 51 states with MW variation, QWI data accessible, SCI publicly available
