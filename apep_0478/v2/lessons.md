## Summary
- **Key insight:** Historical automation papers need to clearly separate descriptive measurement from causal claims
- **What worked well:** Multi-method approach (descriptive panel + SCM + event study + triple-diff) provided converging evidence even when individual methods had limitations
- **What to improve next time:** Include SCM diagnostics (donor weights, RMSPE ratios) from the start; frame displacement regressions as associations from the beginning
